Cherry Blossom Japan

Day 1: Arrival in Kyoto

  • Morning: Arrive at Narita International Airport or Haneda International Airport.
  • Transfer to your hotel in Tokyo.
  • Afternoon: Explore Ueno Park, which is known for its cherry blossoms. Visit the Tokyo National Museum if time allows.
  • Evening: Enjoy dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 2: Cherry Blossom Viewing in Tokyo

  • Morning: Visit Shinjuku Gyoen, a vast park with various types of cherry blossoms.
  • Lunch: Try Japanese street food or dine at a nearby restaurant.
  • Afternoon: Stroll around Chidorigafuchi, where you can rent a rowboat to view cherry blossoms along the moat of the Imperial Palace.
  • Evening: Explore the bustling Shibuya district.

Day 3: Travel to Kyoto

  • Morning: Take the shinkansen (bullet train) to Kyoto.
  • Afternoon: Visit Maruyama Park, a popular cherry blossom spot in Kyoto.
  • Evening: Enjoy a traditional Kyoto-style kaiseki dinner at a local ryokan (traditional Japanese inn).

Day 4: Kyoto Cherry Blossom Viewing

  • Morning: Explore the beautiful Arashiyama Bamboo Grove and visit Tenryu-ji Temple.
  • Lunch: Savor yudofu (tofu hot pot) in Arashiyama.
  • Afternoon: Visit the stunning cherry blossom trees at the Heian Shrine.
  • Evening: Explore the Gion district in the hopes of spotting geisha and maiko.

Day 5: Kyoto's Cherry Blossoms and Departure

  • Morning: Visit Philosopher's Path and its cherry blossoms.
  • Lunch: Enjoy a traditional bento box or a meal at a local restaurant.
  • Afternoon: Explore Kiyomizu-dera and its beautiful cherry blossom views.
  • Evening: Transfer to Kansai International Airport or Osaka International Airport for your departure.
