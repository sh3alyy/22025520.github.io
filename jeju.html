Jeju Island Life

DAY 1: Arrival in Jeju

  • Morning: Arrive at Jeju International Airport.
  • Transfer to your hotel or accommodation.
  • Afternoon: Explore Jeju's beautiful beaches. Visit the famous Hyeopjae Beach for some relaxation and water sports activities.
  • Evening: Enjoy a welcome dinner at a local restaurant featuring Jeju's fresh seafood.

DAY 2: Jeju Sightseeing

  • Morning: Visit the Seongsan Ilchulbong Sunrise Peak, a UNESCO World Heritage site known for its breathtaking views and unique crater. You can hike to the summit.
  • Lunch: Enjoy local cuisine at a nearby restaurant.
  • Afternoon: Explore Manjanggul Cave, one of the largest lava tubes in the world.
  • Evening: Stroll through the charming Seogwipo Olle Market for local street food and shopping.

DAY 3: Cultural Experiences and Departure

  • Morning: Visit the Jeju Folk Village to learn about the island's traditional culture and history.
  • Lunch: Enjoy a traditional Korean meal.
  • Afternoon: Explore Hallasan National Park, home to South Korea's highest peak, Mount Halla. Depending on your group's fitness level, you can choose a trail suitable for your needs.
  • Evening: Transfer to Jeju International Airport for your departure.
